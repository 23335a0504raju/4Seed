About us

Farmer

It is to help farmer ensure greater profitability through direct famer and consumer.The farmers who are eligible are verefied and registered by applying with their require documents.Farmers can communicate with consumer for bargaining the products.Farmers upload their cultivated products in the respective portals.And can do business.


Consumer

Consumers can buy the products in the consumer portal.Consumer get profit for the products by not buying from the dealers.Consumer get the organic products .It keeps them healthy and safety.Consumers get the delivered products by online payment or offline payment.Every consumer receives orders within 3 to 5hrs.


Worker

In the worker portal employment is provided to the people.There are two types of worker backend workers and front end workers.In the backend workers customer care and database management jobs are available. The front workers has to receive products from the farmer and deliver to the customer in time.


Business Tips

Every farmers can solve their problem by checking the business portal.Farmers can check weather conditions for the process of cultivation.It divided into 2 types farming tips and climatic condition tips.
